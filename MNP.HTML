LOCATION

Located 20 km away from the city centre at 983P+ J34, Moinabad, Ranga Reddy District, Chilkoor, Telangana 500075

THINGS TO DO

•Nature Walks: Explore the park's walking trails and enjoy the lush greenery, wildlife, and serene surroundings.
•Bird Watching: Bring your binoculars and spot a wide variety of bird species that call the park home.
•Photography: Capture the park's natural beauty, wildlife, and landscapes through photography.
•Wildlife Spotting: Keep an eye out for the park's inhabitants, which include deer, peacocks, and various reptiles.
•Picnicking: Pack a picnic and enjoy a meal amidst nature at one of the designated picnic spots.
•Meditation and Yoga: The tranquil environment makes it an ideal place for meditation or yoga.
•Butterfly Garden: Visit the butterfly garden within the park to witness colorful butterflies in their natural habitat.
•Environmental Education: Check if the park offers any educational programs or guided tours to learn more about its flora and fauna.
•Nature Interpretation Center: Explore the center to gain insights into the park's ecology and conservation efforts.
•Conservation Awareness: Support the park's conservation efforts by adhering to rules and promoting responsible tourism.
